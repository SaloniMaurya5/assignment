Death

  • Lamarr died in Casselberry, Florida, on January 19, 2000, of heart disease, aged 85.
  • Her son Anthony Loder spread her ashes in Austria's Vienna Woods in accordance with her last wishes.
  • In 2014, a memorial to Lamarr was unveiled in Vienna's Central Cemetery.
